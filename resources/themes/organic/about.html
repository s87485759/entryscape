Organic.Edunet

Organic.Edunet aims to facilitate access, usage and exploitation of digital educational content related to Organic Agriculture (OA) and Agroecology (AE). Confolio is used within Organic.Edunet as a learning repository to collect, harvest and annotate learning resources which are then harvested by the Organic.Edunet Portal.

Confolio

Confolio is an electronic portfolio system based on the resource and metadata management framework SCAM.
It provides a wide range of functionality, such as:

  • Management and advanced description of resources such as uploaded files, links, and non-digital objects
  • Collaborative creation and annotation of resource repositories
  • Integration of other online services such as Flickr, YouTube, Google Calendar, etc
