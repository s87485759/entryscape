H-Net – A multilateral project for Development of Innovation

H-Net is the acronym for the ‘European Network for Harmonization of training in Hematology.’ This project was awarded a grant under the Leonardo da Vinci Lifelong Learning Programme by the European Commission. In a joint effort of 26 partners, H-Net aims to arrive at a truly harmonized curriculum for European hematologists, thus raising the competence levels of hematologists and, ultimately, improving patient care and public health.
